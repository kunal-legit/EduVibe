Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced coaching?

Yes, we provide comprehensive coaching for both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced with specialized materials and test series.

How can I enroll in your institute?

You can enroll by: Visiting our center, Filling out the online application form on our website, Taking an admission test, if required.

What is the teaching methodology?

Our teaching approach includes: Daily interactive lectures, Problem-solving sessions, Weekly tests and performance analysis, One-on-one doubt-solving classes.

What study material will I receive?

Comprehensive study material for all topics, DPPs (Daily Practice Problems), Question banks for JEE Mains and Advanced, Mock test papers.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on: Performance in our admission test, Academic excellence in school, Special concessions for economically weaker sections.

What facilities does your institute provide?

Experienced faculty with expertise in JEE, Well-equipped classrooms, Online and offline test series, Hostel and library facilities for outstation students.

How do you track student progress?

Regular mock tests and assessments, Individual performance reports, Parent-teacher meetings to discuss progress.
